Founding Story

Adopet adalah website adopsi hewan yang dikembangkan akibat kesulitan yang dialami orang sekitar saat melakukan proses adopsi. Adopet berusaha membantu hewan terlantar di sekitar agar mendapat rumah baru bagi mereka.

About Adopet

Adopet is pet adoption web to connect between Adoptee and Adopter. Our pet adoption process is FREE and we are against animal trade. Adopet has several services such as article, vaccine, and donate that help you get to know well about treatment for your pet and donation for street pet.
